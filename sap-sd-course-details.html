SAP SD TRAINING

As one of the best SAP training institutes, RevanSoft provides real-time and placement-oriented SAP training to the aspirants.

What is SAP SD ?

With HRM Training clients can get core accounting and reporting capabilities. SAP FICO ensures the most up-to-date balances and reporting accuracy through complete integration between the General Ledger (GL) and the Accounts Receivable (AR) and Accounts Payable (AP) and Asset Accounts (AA).

The HRM Training module integrates with other modules such as Controlling, Sales and Distribution, Purchasing and Materials Management and Human Resources. Document transactions occurring within these modules generate account postings via account determination tables.
